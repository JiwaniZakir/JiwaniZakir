### What I Build

I design and ship AI systems that operate autonomously in production — multi-agent orchestrations that coordinate across tools and APIs, RAG pipelines that retrieve and reason over heterogeneous data sources, and graph-based intelligence platforms that model complex relationship networks. My work sits at the intersection of applied ML research and systems engineering: implementing papers like ReAct, Self-RAG, and Constitutional AI as production services, then wrapping them in zero-trust security architectures with end-to-end encryption, formal verification, and infrastructure-as-code deployment. Most of what I build runs 24/7 with no human in the loop.
